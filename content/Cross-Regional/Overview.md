# Cross-Regional Overview

## Scope
This section holds thematic notes that cut across regions and traditions.
Use it for comparison, transmission, syncretism, canon formation, ritual patterns, sacred space, authority, cosmology, and method.

- Syncretism
- Scripture, canon, and authority
- Priesthood and ritual specialists
- Pilgrimage and sacred geography
- Myth, cosmology, and afterlife models
- divine triads, kingship myths, and death/rebirth patterns
